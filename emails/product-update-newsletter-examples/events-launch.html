Events is now live

Your community is active. But activity without structure fades. Without structured moments, engagement drifts. Content gets buried. Members scroll, consume, and leave with no real reason to come back.

Events gives you the full infrastructure to turn passive users into a community that keeps showing up. Schedule events, drive RSVPs, go live, and analyze participation, all from within your platform.

What's included

Event Creation & Management

Schedule events directly from the social.plus Console. Configure the name, description, date and time, and choose the format: in-person, digital, or livestream.

Event Discovery

A dedicated events feed where members can browse upcoming events, revisit past ones, and explore what's live right now, both across the platform and within specific communities.

Attendees & RSVP

Members RSVP directly in the app. Organizers get a real-time attendee list to track participation, and automated reminders go out before the event starts.

Livestream, upgraded

Events and Livestream are built to work together. When an event goes live, a Live Now ring appears on member profiles. Invite co-streamers and run multi-stream sessions directly from your platform.

AI Moderation & Analytics

Moderate livestream chats in real time from the Console and UI Kit. Track attendance, engagement, and performance with comprehensive event analytics built into the platform.
