# Link Building Guidelines

Target blog: https://www.social.plus/blog

## Target URL Requirements

### Must Have
- Dofollow links
- Domain Rating (DR) ≥ 50
- Page type: blogs, articles, glossaries only

### Not Allowed
- Homepages, product pages, landing pages, service pages
- Location-specific pages (e.g., "Marketing agency in Dubai")
- Highly niche industry blogs (e.g., "SEO Tips for Law Firms")
- Pages that compete with the article's keywords

## Restricted Categories (Auto-Reject)

Never accept links from:
- Crypto, casino, gambling
- Design/editing software
- Presentation software/templates
- WordPress templates
- Chatbots
- Converter tools
- Brand name/logo generators
- PC/OS cleanup apps
- QR code generators
- Dropshipping
- B2C products
- "Ways to make money" content

## Anchor Text Rules

- No branded anchors (brand names)
- Keep anchor short
- Never in introductions or conclusions
- Must provide genuine reader value (not promotional)

## Text Modification Rules

### Contextual Relevance
- Must align with article topic
- Must fit the specific paragraph context
- Off-topic additions = reject

### Writing Quality
- 100% human-written
- Match article's writing style and tone
- No AI phrases: "Additionally", "Furthermore", "Moreover", "In addition"
- No duplicate text from other websites

## Value Principle

Backlinks must provide additional value to the reader by linking to credible, authoritative, and directly related content. Links that appear promotional or do not enhance the reader's experience will be rejected.
