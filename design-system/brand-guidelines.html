A complete design system for building consistent, premium, and accessible social.plus experiences — across every surface and every team.

01
Logo

The mark

The social.plus logo embeds a plus sign and a subtle chat bubble. A gradient icon mark paired with a white wordmark. Never alter the proportions, colours, or spacing.

On brand black
On light
On black
On surface 1
On light
check_circle Do
  • Use on Brand Black (#111111)
  • Use on dark surfaces (Surface 1–3)
  • Use on white or light grey backgrounds
  • Maintain clearspace equal to the height of the icon mark on all sides
  • Use the full wordmark for primary brand applications
cancel Don't
  • Place the icon mark on Ultramarine — the blue arm disappears
  • Stretch, skew, or rotate the logo
  • Apply drop shadows or glows to the logo
  • Change the internal colour arrangement
  • Place on busy or clashing coloured backgrounds
